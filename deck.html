Greenhouse Gas Emission Insights

Turning greenhouse gas data into clear, actionable insights.

The Challenge

Climate data is vast, fragmented, and difficult to interpret. Decision-makers and educators need simple, powerful tools to uncover the story behind the numbers.

The Solution

An interactive tool with three key modules:

  • Planet Heater — Emissions and warming trends
  • Carbon Chronicles — CO2
  • Greener, Faster — leaders in climate progress

Data & Tools

  • IMF Greenhouse Gas Accounts
  • Python + Pandas
  • Streamlit multipage app
  • Gemini AI (Policy Assistant)

Demo

Insights

  • Energy and transport remain the biggest emitters.
  • Some regions show accelerating declines in specific sectors.
  • Rate of change signals climate leadership.

Value for Stakeholders

  • Policymakers: identify high-impact levers
  • Analysts: fast baseline comparisons
  • Educators: simplify climate education

Roadmap

  • Per-capita analysis
  • Deeper regional drilldowns
  • Forecasting models
